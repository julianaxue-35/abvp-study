ABVP SMP RVS — Mock Exam Day

A blueprint-weighted, timed simulation of the 350-item Combined RVS Exam (two parts, one day) — with a pausable lunch, live pacing, and a Prometric-style sketch pad that hides the question while you calculate.

Set up your exam

The exam is split into two timed sessions with a self-paced lunch between. Questions are drawn randomly and weighted to the 2026 blueprint below. MCQs are auto-graded; flashcards are self-graded (Got it / Missed it). Nothing is marked right/wrong until your results — just like the real exam.

Exam-day tips

  • Pace: at ~1 min/question you must average steady progress. The bar at the top tells you live whether you're On pace, Ahead (slow down — you may be rushing), or Behind (speed up).
  • Flag & move on. Don't burn 3 minutes on one item — flag it, answer your best guess, and return via the palette (≡) if time allows. Unanswered = wrong; a guess is never worse.
  • Calculations: open the Sketch pad for metrics/biostats items. It covers the question while open (as in the real exam) — read and note the numbers first, then sketch.
  • 3-option style: the real ABVP stems are clinical vignettes, often a/b/c. Read the lead-in carefully (it sometimes completes the sentence).
  • Eat and hydrate at the lunch break — you control when it ends.
